ARHunt

Adding that extra piece of treasure to your everyday life.

Game description

ARHunt is an Augmented Reality treasure hunting game for iOS, running in the Argon web browser. ARHunt will let you explore your surroundings for virtual treasure chests containing unimaginable riches.

All throughout the world of ARHunt virtual tresure chests are hidden that can only be opened by you, once you have found the matching key. By looking at the real world through the camera on the back of your phone you will be able to find virtual objects hidden next to real life objects. You will be given clues as to what object you will need to look for to find your tresure. When you've found the object with the camera on your phone, the game will show a virtual key or chest in the room in front of that object. Once you've found a matching pair of key and chest the tresure will be yours.

In this demo you will be looking for a tresure hidden in the VIC studio. To find the key you will have to look for one of the oldest air crafts ever invented. The tresure chest is hidden by the pirates' colorful animal companion.
